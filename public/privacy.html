Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At Bacaro Budget Manager, we believe that your financial data is personal and should remain private. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, protect, and handle your data when you use our application and its accompanying features, such as the Kwarta AI engine.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect data that is essential for the functionality of Bacaro:

  • Account Information:Email address, username, and password (encrypted).
  • Financial Data:Transactions, wallet balances, and savings goals that you manually input.
  • Preferences:Choice of theme (Dark/Light mode), language, and currency display settings.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is strictly used to empower your personal budget management experience. Specific uses include:

  • Calculating net worth, monthly cash flow, and spending habits.
  • Providing AI-driven financial insights and advice viaKwarta AI.
  • Enabling data exports in CSV format for your personal bookkeeping.

Strict Privacy Rule:We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal or financial data to outside parties. Your data is stored securely and is only accessible by you via your authenticated account.

3. AI Analytics (Kwarta AI)

When you interact with Kwarta AI, your transaction summaries and goal data may be processed to generate helpful responses. These interactions are private and are used solely to enhance the accuracy of the AI's suggestions for your account.

4. Data Security & Retention

We use robust security protocols, including SSL encryption and secure password hashing, to protect your data. We retain your information as long as your account is active. You may use the "Delete Account" feature in Settings at any time to permanently remove all your data from our servers.

5. Cookie Policy

Bacaro uses local storage and limited cookies to maintain your login session and save your UI preferences (like Dark Mode). These do not track your activity on other websites.

6. Your Consent

By using Bacaro, you consent to our privacy policy as outlined here. We reserve the right to update this policy to reflect changes in our service or regulatory requirements.

7. Third-Party Services

Kwarta AI is powered by a third-party artificial intelligence provider (DeepSeek API). When you use the AI chat feature, summarized transaction and goal data may be sent to this provider to generate responses. No personally identifiable information (such as your name, email, or password) is ever shared with third-party AI services. Only anonymized financial summaries are transmitted, and these are not stored by the third-party provider beyond the duration of the request.

8. Children's Privacy

Bacaro Budget Manager is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that a user is under 18, we will take steps to delete their account and associated data promptly.
